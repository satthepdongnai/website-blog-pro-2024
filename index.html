Introduction to Sắt Thép Biên Hòa

Sắt Thép Biên Hòa is a leading steel supplier in Vietnam, recognized for providing high-quality steel products to a diverse range of industries. Located in Đồng Nai province, our company is dedicated to delivering superior steel materials, including steel pipes, square steel tubes, rectangular steel tubes, stainless steel pipes, and various other steel products. With a commitment to quality, competitive pricing, and exceptional customer service, Sắt Thép Biên Hòa has established itself as a trusted partner in the construction and industrial sectors.

Our Product Range

At Sắt Thép Biên Hòa, we offer a wide array of steel products designed to meet the needs of various applications:

  • Steel Pipes: Our steel pipes are available in different grades and sizes, suitable for structural, mechanical, and industrial applications. We provide both galvanized and black steel pipes, ensuring durability and resistance to corrosion, making them ideal for both indoor and outdoor projects.
  • Square Steel Tubes: Our square steel tubes are known for their strength and versatility. Available in various dimensions and thicknesses, they are widely used in construction, fabrication, and manufacturing sectors. The high tensile strength and uniform shape make them perfect for frameworks, supports, and fencing.
  • Rectangular Steel Tubes: These tubes are highly valued for their strength and flexibility in design, suitable for a range of structural applications. They are commonly used in construction, transportation, and agricultural machinery, offering robust performance and long-lasting durability.
  • Stainless Steel Pipes and Tubes: We supply a wide variety of stainless steel pipes and tubes that provide excellent resistance to rust and corrosion. These are ideal for applications where hygiene and cleanliness are paramount, such as in the food and beverage, pharmaceutical, and petrochemical industries.
  • Flanges and Pipe Fittings: Our extensive range of flanges and pipe fittings are designed to complement our steel pipes, ensuring secure and leak-proof connections in any pipeline system. These fittings are manufactured to meet the highest quality standards, providing reliable performance in even the most demanding environments.
